Employee Scheduler

A REAL TIME SCHEDULING APP created for a tutoring company so they can be able to schedule employees more efficiently. This was a 4 person group project in MEAN where we used MySQL for the database for ease of table relations / associations. Employers are able to log in, add, edit, and delete employees and shifts. Employees are able to edit and change days they are available for shifts. Employers can as well and are also able to categorize employees on their job position. Employers are able to assign shifts to employees as well as see who is able to take the shift according to their availability and the category.
